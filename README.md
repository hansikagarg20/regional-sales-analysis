# Regional Sales Analysis — XYZ Co. (2014–2018)

## Overview
End-to-end sales analysis of 28,000+ B2B orders across products, channels, and US regions.

## Tools
Python · pandas · matplotlib · seaborn · scipy · statsmodels

## Key Findings
- Revenue flat at ~$295–299M/year (2014–2017) — growth has plateaued
- Export channel carries statistically higher profit margin than Wholesale (p < 0.001)
- Bulk orders (3.44% of orders) drive 11.6% of revenue — a segment worth protecting
- Customer concentration risk is low — 74% of customers needed to reach 80% of revenue

- `Regional_Sales_Analysis_Updated.ipynb` — full analysis notebook
- `Regional_Sales_Dataset.xlsx` — source data
